Four lines. Infinite depth.

I

The Heart Line

How you feel and how you love

The deepest horizontal line on your palm speaks to emotional intelligence, attachment patterns, and how you experience connection. AstraPalm tracks how its energy shifts across seasons.

II

The Head Line

How you think and decide

Your head line reveals your cognitive style, decision-making instincts, and the quality of your clarity on any given day. This line is the most responsive to your mental state.

III

The Life Line

Your vitality and resilience

Contrary to popular belief, the life line does not predict how long you live. It shows how fully you inhabit your life, your physical energy, and your capacity for recovery.

IV

The Fate Line

Your sense of purpose

Not everyone has a strong fate line. If you do, it speaks to the pull you feel toward something larger than daily routine. AstraPalm uses this to calibrate your purpose-related readings.
